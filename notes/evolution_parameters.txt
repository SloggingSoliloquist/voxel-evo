If the number of species is too small: Too much competition, lack of         
diversity as winners disproportionately dominate.

If the number of species is too large; Too little species, no competition
at all. No actual change ends up happening between generations. 

important parameters: 
Stagnation is related to species, to evaluate whether a species needs to be
eliminated i.e go extinct or not. 
Max Stagnation is essentially the number of generations of no improvement
that it takes for a species to be considered stagnant and hence removed. 

Species elitism is essentially the nymber of species that are protected
from stagnations. This is essentually to prevent mass extinction before new
species can arirse. Eg. a species elitism of 3 will prevent the 3 highest
fitness species from being removed regardless of whetheer they 
have corssed ths tagnation threshold or not. 

The Default Reproduction Section is essentially settings that aren't specific
to species. All the above was required only cos you want to have different
species in your population.
